Elon had a tendency to call people stupid. How could I possibly blame that child?"[32] After the attack, Elon was enrolled in private school.[31][32] Elon was an enthusiastic reader of books, later attributing his success in part to having read The Lord of the Rings, the Foundation series, and The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy.[17][33] At age ten, he developed an interest in computing and video games, teaching himself how to program from the VIC-20 user manual.[34] At age twelve, Elon sold his BASIC-based game Blastar to PC and Office Technology magazine for approximately $500.[35][36]
